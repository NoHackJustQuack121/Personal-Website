About Me

My name is Naoki Izumi, and I am currently a 10th-grade student at The Browning School. I am an active and motivated individual who balances academics with extracurricular commitments.

I have been a competitive swimmer since elementary school. Balancing a demanding training schedule with schoolwork has taught me discipline, perseverance, and strong time-management skills.

In addition to athletics, I am involved in school clubs. I am a publisher for The Gryett, Browning’s student newspaper, and an active member of the Mock Trial club.

Through these experiences, I have learned how to manage multiple responsibilities while maintaining focus and quality. I look forward to continuing my education and personal growth.
